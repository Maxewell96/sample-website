REV. FR. PROF. ANTHONY AFFUL BRONI

A BRIEF HISTORY OF REV. FR. PROF. ANTHONY AFFUL BRONI


The Governing Council of the University of Education, Winneba, has in a Press Release appointed Rev. Fr. Professor Anthony Afful-Broni as the substantive Vice-Chancellor of the University with immediate effect for a limited period of 3 years, ending 30th September, 2021. Rev. Fr. Prof. Anthony Afful-Broni holds a PhD in Educational Administration and Management and a Masters in Pastoral Counselling from New York City’s Fordham University.


SOME HIGHLIGHT OF HIS CAREER


An ordained Catholic priest of the Archdiocese of Cape Coast for 30 years, Fr. Afful-Broni serves on many boards nationally and internationally. Immediate past President of the Priests’ Senate, and former Chairman of the Education Committee of the Archdiocese of Cape Coast. Member of the College of Consultors for the Catholic Archdiocese of Cape Coast. A past University Chaplain of the University of Education, Winneba (UEW). Served as Pro-Vice-Chancellor for two consecutive terms. Dean of the Faculty of Educational Studies for five years. The Editor-in-Chief of the International Journal of Educational Leadership. Former Consultant to the Academic Quality Assurance Unit of the University of Ghana, Legon. A member of several professional bodies including: Kappa Delta Pi Phi Delta Kappa the Association for Supervision and Curriculum Development (ASCD), USA for 20 years
